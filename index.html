What is Flexbox?

Flexbox products are a breakthrough technology that render old-fashioned boxes a thing of the past. Infusing naturally harvested materials with a proprietery alloy of Nitinol, Flexbox products can readily adapt to virtually any shape and size. Gone are the days of buying expensive cardboard boxes and hoping items will fit. Instead of items to fit your boxes, Flexible Box Inc. has created boxes to fit your items.

Specifications

Ecologically Conscious

All Flexbox products are virtually emissions-free, biodegradable, and comprised of environmentally friendly materials

Ethically Sourced

Occurring in natural deposits, Flexbox components are harvested from the Glowing Box Mines of the Nepeta Cataria Region

Wireless Connectivity

Allowing for seamless integration, every Flexbox can be paired to communicate remotely with SMART enabled rotary telephones, pepper grinders, and wood-fired pizza ovens

Bespoke Pesonalization

Every Flexbox is handmade, by robots, for each customer; this process allows for an unprecedented level of customization, finally providing individuals the ability to truly define themselves by a material posession
